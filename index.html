Research
NLP for Dementia Care: Communication Assistance
JSS Academy of Technical Education, Bengaluru. Exploring how NLP can support language challenges in individuals with dementia.

The Problem

Dementia affects language recall, sentence formation, and communication ability. While many assistive technologies focus on monitoring and safety, fewer address communication support and language assistance for patients experiencing cognitive decline.

My Contribution

  • Conducted literature review and technical research into NLP-based communication assistance
  • Evaluated existing dementia support technologies and identified gaps
  • Contributed to research synthesis and documentation for the study

Associated Publication

NLP Based Word Predictor for Dementia Patients: A Systematic Review
2022 International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Control Systems (ICICCS), IEEE.

Lasting Impact

This experience seeded my interest in healthcare AI and human-centered ML. The instinct that AI should assist and not replace, that language is more than text, that usability matters as much as accuracy. That thread runs through MedEar, through the burnout recognition system, through everything I build now.
